The Concept

Every semester thousands of college students buy books, usually used, at around 70% of the retail price. When semester ends, the same books are sold at around 35% of retail price, which again are sold to the next batch of students at 70%. The vendors get to keep around 35% of the margin every semester. Add to that, the hassle it is to buy / sell at vendors terms.

Why bear with this crap, when you students can buy and sell amongst yourselves in the same college and at much better prices?

Best Prices

Buy and sell books at 50% of the retail price. Simple as that!

Peace of Mind

Buy from and sell to students in your own college with trust.

Most Convenient

Pick up and drop off right in your college!
